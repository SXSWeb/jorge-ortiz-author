About Us

SXSWeb Design

Our names are Bekah and Jorge, and we are the owners of SXSWeb Design, and Dance-Virtual. During college, Jorge learned to program websites from scratch, because that was all that was available. He saw many years of technology come and go. Bekah was a ballroom dancer, and started learning technology while she was pregnant. Together, we learned wild things while running a small business. We now use what we learned about human-computer interactions to elevate other small businesses through carefully crafted websites.

When we started our own business, we didn't have $3,000 (plus $50/hour for edits) of capital to invest in a website that would attract customers. Many small businesses are in a similar situation. We realized even the businesses that do have money to spend upfront, pay a large corporation to get a cookie-cutter website that fails to attract their customers.

We provide a business model to fit your website needs. You start with $0 down, and $150 a month. It's more manageable for small businesses to get started with us. Sometimes getting started is all it takes.
