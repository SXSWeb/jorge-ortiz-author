About the Author

Building Mariel's World

Assassin's Nocturne began as a question: what does it cost an elf raised for war to choose her own path? Three books later, that question is still being answered — in blood, in ash, and in the coils of an old throne.

Blade of Shadows is out now. Ash and Blood and Crown of Serpents continue Mariel's story.
